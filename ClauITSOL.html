About NexaBridge

Where Business Strategy Meets Technology Clarity

Our Story

Built by BAs, for Business

NexaBridge was born from a simple frustration: too many technology projects fail not because of bad code, but because of unclear requirements. Our founders — seasoned BAs from the banking and manufacturing sectors — set out to fix that.


Today, our team of 45 certified Business Analysts operates across APAC, EMEA, and the Americas, serving clients from fast-scaling startups to FTSE 250 enterprises. Every engagement begins and ends with one mission: making sure IT delivers exactly what the business needs.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services, process, and engagement model.

IT Business Analysis is the practice of identifying and documenting business needs, then translating them into clear requirements that IT teams can build against. Without it, technology projects frequently deliver solutions that don't match what the business actually needed — leading to rework, cost overruns, and frustrated stakeholders. Our BAs act as the critical bridge between your business strategy and your technology teams.

Unlike general IT consultancies who cover everything from infrastructure to development, we are specialists. Every consultant at NexaBridge is a trained, certified Business Analyst. This focus means deeper expertise, more rigorous methods, and better outcomes specifically in the analysis and requirements space. We complement your existing IT partners rather than replacing them.

We have active experience across Financial Services, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Retail, Government, Education, Energy, Logistics, and Professional Services. While our BA methods are transferable, we prioritise building domain knowledge so our analysts speak your language from day one.

All of the above. Our BAs are trained across Agile (Scrum, SAFe, Kanban), traditional Waterfall, and hybrid approaches. We adapt our documentation and facilitation style to match your delivery methodology rather than forcing a template-based approach.

Engagements vary significantly depending on scope. A targeted requirements audit might take 1–2 weeks. A full business process redesign programme can run 3–6 months. Digital transformation BA support is often ongoing over 12–24 months. We scope each engagement precisely and offer fixed-fee proposals wherever possible to give you cost certainty.

Yes. All our senior consultants hold internationally recognised IIBA® certifications (CBAP® or CCBA®). Many also hold additional certifications in Agile, Six Sigma, TOGAF, or Prince2 depending on their specialisation. We are an IIBA Endorsed Education Provider and actively invest in continuous professional development.

We deliver effectively both remotely and on-site, and many of our engagements combine both. For requirements workshops and stakeholder sessions we often prefer in-person facilitation, but our remote collaboration toolkit (Miro, Zoom, Teams, Confluence) enables excellent outcomes when travel is not practical.

Our complimentary consultation is a 60-minute session with a senior BA. We review your current challenge, explore the scope, and give you honest advice on the right approach — whether that involves NexaBridge or not. There is no sales pressure, and we'll provide a written summary of our recommendations within 48 hours.

All engagements are governed by a signed NDA from day one. We are ISO 27001 aware and follow strict data handling protocols. Client documentation is stored in encrypted, access-controlled environments and is never shared outside the engagement team without explicit written consent.
